FAQs

What is Hackster?

Hackster is a 9-hour hackathon that brings together creative minds to solve real-world problems through technology. It's a platform for innovation, learning, and collaboration.

Who can participate?

Any student currently enrolled in a college/university can participate. Both undergraduate and postgraduate students are welcome.

What's the team size?

Teams can have 2-4 members including. Solo participation is not allowed.

Is it free to participate?

Yes, participation is completely free! We believe in making innovation accessible to everyone.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, charger, student ID, and lots of enthusiasm! We'll provide the rest.

Are there participation certificates?

Yes, all participants who successfully submit their projects before the deadline will receive a participation certificate while winners will receive a winner's achievement certificate.
